ABOUT

HEAL WITH US is a foundation found in 2019. HEAL WITH US is an accepting Cancer Support Group.
Everything we do is founded in our core belief that no one should ever feel they are alone.
We all have our own life’s journey, but the path shouldn’t be a lonely one.
Our commitment is to provide a safe space for sharing, listening, learning, and supporting each other.We assist you in learning to cope
with the cancer diagnosis ensuring you feel less overwhelmed and more in control. We help you explore the meaning of your cancer experience,
gather additional information concerning that type of cancer and take you through important decisions.
also, we assist you in managing tough emotions such as anger, fear, guilt, depression, and anxiety,
as well as symptoms of pain and fatigue.
